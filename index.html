ABOUT

Hi there! I love all things geology and baking. I am currently living in Lakewood Colorado and love to go visit the mountains. I have diploma in baking and pastry and specialize in pastries and cake decorating as my main hobby. I am always trying new recipes and am constanly looking for the next new dessert to try and recreate. Check out my instagram for some of my best creations!
Instagram Link

I have previously been working as a geologist doing image interpretation and geosteering. I have worked in various basins to provide customized interpretations and reports with both real time data and post drilling log data. I complete projects from start to finish from data processing and analysis to printing and formatting to presentation and delivery.

After being laid off (again) I am currently working toward a certificate in Data Analysis, Programming Languages and Front-end Web Visualization through the University of Denver. I am hoping these new skills will lead me to an opportunity that is a bit more stable than my past positions have been. This bio website is part of that as I am learning HTML/CSS and JavaScript as part of this course.

I also enjoy volunteering at Dinosaur Ridge in my free time. Dinosaur ridge is the top rated dinosaur track site in the US and has lots of fossils and geology. I do everything from leading guided tours such as walk with a geologist, teaching certain stations on the ridge during special events to being a docent in the exhibit hall museum. If you are in the area I definitely recommend checking it out
